1. How do I create an account?
1. How do I create an account?

On the home page, there is a sign up button right below the login in screen. Click this button and enter in the requested information to create an account.

2. Is there a cost to sign up?
2. Is there a cost to sign up?

No, creating an account is completely free.

3.Can I delete my account if I no longer use it?
3.Can I delete my account if I no longer use it?

Yes, after logging in, go to the account settings tabs and click delete account. A request will be made to remove all the information associated with your account and an email will be sent to verify the deletion.

4.What can I do if I forget my password?
4.What can I do if I forget my password?

After a failed attempt to login in, a link will appear asking if you forgot your password. Click the link and enter in your email adress and a email will be sent to the address to reset your password.
